C# Gamejam Game

Using C# alongside Unity, I made this game in two days with a team of 4 people in under 2 days. This was for a KSU GameJam. This project tested my ability to work with a diverse team under an intense deadline. The game itself is a simple city exploration simulator using 2D sprites rendered on 3D environments.
