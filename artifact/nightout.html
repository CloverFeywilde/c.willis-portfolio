NightOut- KSU Gamejam

Night Out is a game that I helped create during a 48 hour gamejam with a team of 4. I worked on the character animations, painted 3D textures, and organized all of the art assets and prepared them for the programmers to use. Coordination and effective communication was imperitive when working with a team under such a tight deadline. With such differing skillsets between the 4 of us, the biggest challenge was communicating with one another and bringing together our individual efforts into a systematic pipeline.
