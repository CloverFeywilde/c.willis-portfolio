Bluejay
The captivating bust of a melodic mermaid soothes her admirers into a into a sensational trance. Her mysterious aura and alluring demeanor melts reason and lures bodies to a distant land below the surface.

Iwanted to create an alluring bust image by focusing less on the figure and more on facial details and style. I played with the idea of using water to suggest something more beyond the frame, while focusing on defining her facial features and honing my art-style. I chose an analogous color palette and left most the contrast to be done via color saturation and composition. Her scales presented the most challenge, as I had to paint and blend them one by one.
