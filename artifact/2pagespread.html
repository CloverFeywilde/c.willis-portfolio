Print Magazine 2-Page Spread

I wanted to hone my visual design skills and create a 2 page layout like one might find in a print magazine. I made heavy use of the female form as a design element with this project, as I wanted to immediately “wow” the viewer with captivating imagery that would command their attention. The layout adheres to the golden ratio, and thus has a pleasing rhythm, balance, asymmetry, and a strong sense of hierarchy. Lastly, I wanted to add context and relevance to the large image of the models by putting in a thought provoking pull quote from the article. The viewer’s eye is then led to the title, and then enters the body text via the large drop cap on the first line. All of the writing, photography, and visual design was done by myself. The article itself features myself as a model photographer and goes into detail about my work, process, and creative vision.
