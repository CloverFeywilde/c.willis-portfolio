Article on Sensuality

This document is a creative writing piece on sensuality and femininity that aims to challenge people’s preconceptions and learned discomfort. I treated the layout of the document as a design problem of it’s own, and kept it down to a single page with no distracting color. With minimal visual elements present, every design decision I made affected the document profusely.

...every design decision I made affected the document profusely.
The typography I chose by pairing sans-serif headers with serif body text. Using my photography and photoshop skills, I took a captivating image of a model that I had previously photographed and edited it over a brush stroke. The stroke was designed to tease the eye by highlighting some of the model’s captivating features and leaving others to suggestion. A pull quote for such a short document would have made little sense, so I opted instead to set the tone of the document by using a dictionary cutout of the topic, “sensuality”.
