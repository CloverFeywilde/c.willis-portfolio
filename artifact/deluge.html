Deluge- Horror Adventure

Deluge is a web based game that I created from scratch, with no game engines or rendering libraries, during a 2 week Summer Gamejam held on itch.io. I was a solo team and created the game without any outside help. Deluge is a short choose your own adventure game with 5 different endings plus an additional secret ending. I used Coffeescript, a javascript syntax that cleaner and easier to read, along with a little bit of Jquery and HTML5 to build the game.

...creating an entire game by myself in under 2 weeks, while holding down a full time day job, is no simple task.

All background & character art I drew and painted myself; the story was also written by me as well. This project was a testament to how far I'm willing to go to see a vision I have through to its conclusion, as creating an entire game by myself in under 2 weeks, while holding down a full time day job, is no simple task. It's my ambition to revisit this project upon FaerieFM's conclusion, and add greater polish and far more content.
