Eos- Character Concept

Meet Eos, an energetic, fun loving, and easygoing faerie girl. Sheâ€™s the heroine of a videogame in the making called Faerie Heart. The concept art represented here shows both her final design, as well as snippets of the in-depth process that involved: silhouetting, thumbnailing, color and detail exploration, and a final rendering. I worked rapidly in photoshop, letting my imagination run free and working from a mood board of reference images. I created interesting shapes with a black brush and paid little no attention to detail. Over time, I slowly narrowed my options, combined features, and continued refining until I came up with a silhouette that I felt best represented Eos. I did the same with a white brush and began filling in details, eventually moving on to the final step, which involved rendering and experimenting with color. This process required a range of skills, but my foundational graphic design skills truly came into play here like no other.
