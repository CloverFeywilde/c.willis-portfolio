Selene- Character Concept

Meet Selene, a sexy, bold, straight talking, and faerie. She’s the friend, mentor, and rival to the main heroine of an in progress video game project called Faerie Heart. Selene’s design, at a glance, is meant to embody beauty, intimidating skill, and otherworldly allure.

Selene’s design, at a glance, is meant to embody beauty, intimidating skill, and otherworldly allure.
I wanted Selene’s design to feel foreign and exotic, contrasting visually to Eos. Selene was designed using a process that involved silhouettes, thumbnails, color/detail refinement, and a final rendering. I was greatly inspired by the loose fitting and tattered clothing of eastern swordsmen. I made heavy use of contrast as I used my inspiration and contrasted it with sexy tight fitting mesh that showed off a lot of skin. The main problem I had with this was knowing when to stop, and deciding on how much of Selene I needed to censor. I kept her design mostly uncensored, as I decided that it was appropriate for the target audience, and would help with contrast.
