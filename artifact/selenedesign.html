Selene- Character Concept

Meet Selene, a sexy, bold, straight talking, and common sense faerie. She’s meant to serve as the friend, mentor, and rival to the main heroine of an in progress video game project called Faerie Heart. Faerie heart is an east meets west fantasy RPG video game with an all female cast.

having her outfit more revealing helped with making her appear foreign and exotic, adding contrast to the full cast of characters.
Selene’s design, at a glance, is meant to embody beauty, intimidating skill, and otherworldly allure. I wanted her design to feel slightly foreign and exotic to viewers, and thus for her to provide contrast to the main heroine. Selene went through the same design process as Eos, the main heroine, and was made using silhouettes, thumbnails, color/detail refinement, and much more. I was greatly inspired by the loose fitting and tattered clothing of eastern swordsmen. I made heavy use of contrast as I used my inspiration and contrasted it with sexy tight fitting mesh that showed off a lot of skin. The main problem I had with this was knowing when to stop, and deciding on how much of Selene I needed to censor. I took a more liberal approach with her design, as I decided that, her audience wouldn’t mind, and having her outfit more revealing helped with making her appear foreign and exotic, adding contrast to the full cast of characters.
