Lushbox Mobile Interface Design

This is a prototype for a mobile app and a mock company where subscribers customize their own bathroom supply box. I came up with the idea after running out of contact solution, and realizing how often I'm forgetting. Inspired by the Dollar Shave Club's targeted marketing, I decided to go with a similar approach, but instead market primarily to females. I chose soft cool colors to offer a calming solution, and used purple and green for subtle hints of femininity. I also utilized a casual and cutesy script for the headers to appeal to young women who consume a lot of beauty supplies. The sample user icon and logo were designed and illustrated by me. I worked off of the idea that a subscriber can create their own avatar, and have their avatar's face appear on the box that is sent to them each month. The ideas for this project were endless, and what started off as simple practice for a UX wireframe, ended up being an entire design and brand identity project.
