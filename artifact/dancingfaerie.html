Dancing Faerie
When the rest of the world has lost it’s color, I’ll be the brush that paints it all again. I’ll be the one that leads the way. I’m the soft glow in your heart that’ll never go out, and I’ll always guide you no matter the pain. Even when you’re not awake.

The purpose of this piece was to just let myself go and draw creatively something that I wanted to express. It was as much of an exercise to define myself as an artist as it was to simply keep on practicing and creating things. The main challenge to this piece was composition and contrast. Making sure the faerie didn’t get lost in the painterly background was a challenge that I remedied by surrounding her in a complementary colored glow. The background I painted stroke by stroke, reminiscent of a Van Gogh classic. I’ve found that I want the world to be a better place, and just like the faerie here, I want to fill the world with beauty, magic, and optimism.
