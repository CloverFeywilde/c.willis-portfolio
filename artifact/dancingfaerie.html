Dancing Faerie
When the world’s lost it’s color, I’ll be the brush that paints it again. I’ll be the one that leads the way. I’m the soft glow in your heart that’ll never go out, and I’ll always guide you, even when you’re not awake.

The purpose of this piece was to unleash my creativity, and convey something important and relevant to me. It was as much of an exercise to define myself as it was to create a stunning piece. Composition and contrast presented design problems, as the small faerie began to get lost in my Van Gogh-esq background that I painted stroke by stroke. As a solution, I surrounded her in a yellow glow. I’ve found that I want the world to be a better place, and just like the faerie here, I want to fill the world with beauty, magic, and optimism.
