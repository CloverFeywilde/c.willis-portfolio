Feature Website for Nude Models

Photography is my passion, and it has allowed me to work with women that do nude modeling. Despite their hard work and dedication, they’re consistently met with judgement, and ridicule. I made this feature website to help humanize these women and rebrand their craft in a professional and more relatable context.

Balancing provocative imagery with lengthy feature text left me with the delicate task of walking on razor’s edge.

Balancing provocative imagery with lengthy feature text left me with the delicate task of walking on razor’s edge. I designed the content carefully, as an excess of text would keep people from reading the articles, and too much intense imagery could offend them. I wanted viewers to immediately recognize the document as a trustworthy source, and did this by formatting the feature site like a print newspaper. I used white space, pull-quotes, and drop-caps liberally, choosing baskerville as my typeface to help further convey maturity.
