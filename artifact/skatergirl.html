Skatergirl
Ideas weigh heavier than the objects they’re bound to, and I can no longer determine what’s real and what’s not. So which is more real, the girl or the boy?

What started off as a basic exercise in drawing two characters together, turned into a conceptual thought experiment. In this illustration, the girl is standing separately from a boy that she’s painted on the wall. The piece explores the idea of “representation” through the different layers of iconography and aims to blur the concept of reality. I had to research how graffiti artist from the 70’s - 90’s created and designed their respective tags, as well as draft the girl’s long board so it would remain neat, orderly, and hard edged.
