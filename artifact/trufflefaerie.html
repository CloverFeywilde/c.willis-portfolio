Truffle Faerie
...her breath becomes unsteady, and her eyes flutter lightly... Nuzzling comfortably, a warm smile forms across her face, “Why hello there sir. I’ve noticed you prattling about here and there. It’s a pleasure to see you once more,” she says, smiling warmly.

This is the Truffle Faerie, she’s the main character in an interactive story that I wrote. Playing the role of an enigmatic forest guardian, the truffle faerie is as wise of a creature as she is fickle. One of the main challenges to this artwork was creating such a complex background. I wanted the color scheme to push the idea of a mysterious forest at dusk, and convey mixed emotions of uncertainty, mystery, and trance. I enveloped her alluring body in a soft glow to help her dark skin stand out. She’s represented in a low angle and with voluptuous features to both empower and and convey an inviting softness to her personality.
