Model Photo Sale Advertisement

Ihelped a model create and sell one of a kind, individually signed polaroid photos. It helped reinforce her authenticity as a model, and let her fans interact with her work in a unique way. We traveled to South Georgia and shot on a private farm at fall’s height. I used over 3 packs of polaroid film from shooting her in a variety of different compositions and angles, and hand painted apple crates for her to pose on for added visual interest.

To get the word out and strengthen her reach over social media, I brought my digital camera and took some shots to help advertise the photo sale. I kept the vintage look by using a mirrorless sony camera body paired with a few vintage lenses from the 1980’s. While this design problem isn’t the typical setting that a visual designer finds themselves in, it required and benefitted from my design skills.
