Specialty Foods Business Card Redesign

Aclient wanted an improved business card for his specialty foods business. He was very specific on what information he wanted on his card, so the biggest challenge was working under tight limitations. He wanted more information on his business card than I would have suggested, so fitting everything on the card without it looking too busy was a careful balancing act. We went with a 2 sided card design in order to keep the information from being too compact, and this allowed for much greater flexibility. I utilized negative space as a design element, and made sure to balance the text with an image that would both be eye catching and that would give viewers an idea of what his business did at a glance. The design was eastern inspired, and I made use of a thin, elegant typeface, contrasted with a bold stroke around his portrait, reminiscent of a japanese calligraphy brush stroke.
