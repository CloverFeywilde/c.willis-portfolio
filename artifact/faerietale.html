Faerie Tale

Creating slice of life illustrations and telling stories visually is my strength. To get a better idea of how dialogue might go between these two characters, I wanted to visually express them in a scene together. Creating a scene with multiple characters involves visually balancing weight, composition, and pose. The biggest design problem I faced was starting with rough character sketches and developing them into a fully rendered composition. I created the background in a minimalist watercolor style, and was challenged with economy, and knowing when the piece was finished. I painted the piece using light colors, and made use of the white canvas underneath. The scene I wanted to capture a touching moment of friendship between the two characters, as one reads to the other at sunrise.
