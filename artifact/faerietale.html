Faerie Tale

Creating slice of life illustrations and telling stories with my work is something that I’ve found is a strength of mine. With this piece, I wanted to visually express two character’s emotions and interactions with one another so that I could get a better idea of how dialogue between the two might go in a story.

...creating the background in a minimalist watercolor style presented the problem: “How much is too little?”.
The two characters, Eos and Selene, were both designed separately by myself for an in progress fantasy video game called “Faerie Heart”. Creating a composition with multiple characters interacting is a tricky balance between doing double the work, with the added task of keeping both character’s from feeling too “separate” from the composition as a whole. The biggest design problem I faced was converting each of these character’s concept images into a fully rendered and colored piece, complete with color and a background. In addition, creating the background in a minimalist watercolor style presented the problem: “How much is too little?”. The color scheme for the entire thing was painted with light colors, utilizing the white of the canvas underneath. I wanted to make the scene a touching moment of friendship between the two characters, as one reads to the other at sunrise.
