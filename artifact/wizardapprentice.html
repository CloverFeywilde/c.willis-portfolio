Wizard's Apprentice

Creating a mood is an essential skill that an illustrator needs to have. When I create a full composition, each and every element on the page I make contribute to the mood and the theme of the piece. Any elements of my composition that are allowed to stray, invite frustration to the viewer’s eye. I had a very specific vision for this piece, and I wanted to convey that vision using a character alongside an atmospheric background setting. The resulting image is a slice of life fantasy illustration of a student pouring over his grimoire late into the night. The background I kept a careful balance between detailed, and tightly boxed in, with the resulting image showing just enough to give the viewers the idea of what’s going on. The piece also hints at something more beyond the bounds of the image itself.
